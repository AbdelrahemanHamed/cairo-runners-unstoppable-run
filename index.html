Cairo Runners proudly presents "The Unstoppable- Vol 4" in partnership with Helm, now in its 4th edition. The Unstoppable lines with our commitment to inclusivity and our belief in the limitless potential of every individual.

"The Unstoppable Run" goes beyond being just a run; it is a powerful testament to our firm belief in creating a world where everyone, regardless of their abilities, can actively participate and thrive. We are dedicated to providing a safe and empowering environment that encourages individuals to push their boundaries and become a part of something greater.

In this event, participants with disabilities will share a race number and be paired with runners to ensure their safety and ensure a successful run.

Moreover, this event serves as a platform to raise awareness about disabilities. Our partners will have the opportunity to deliver impactful speeches on inclusion and empowerment, further emphasizing the importance of embracing diversity in our society.





Location: Cairo Stadium
Distance: 2KM per lap
Date: 22/12/2023
Time: 8 AM
